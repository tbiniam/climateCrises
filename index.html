Act Now for Our Planet

The climate crisis is a global emergency. Rising temperatures, extreme weather, and biodiversity loss are serious issues we must act on. Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. The crisis comes from the rapid, human-driven rise in greenhouse gases, leading to extreme weather, sea-level rise, and environmental degradation.
